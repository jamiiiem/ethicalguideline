Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an AI chatbot involve ensuring that the chatbot is accessible and equitable to all users, regardless of their background, demographics, or abilities. It encompasses the elimination of biases and the provision of equal treatment in the chatbot's interactions, promoting diversity and accessibility.

G1 Bias Mitigation

Definition:

Developers should be reducing or eliminating biases in the chatbot’s responses and decision-making processes to ensure fairness.

Techniques:

  • Bias Detection Algorithms: Implement algorithms that automatically detect and flag biased content or decisions.
  • Diverse Training Data: Use diverse datasets that represent a wide range of demographics and perspectives during training to minimize inherent biases.
  • Regular Audits: Conduct regular bias audits of the chatbot’s performance to identify and address any emerging biases.
