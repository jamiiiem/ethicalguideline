Section C: Responsibility

The Responsibility section focuses on ensuring that the AI chatbot behaves ethically and responsibly in all interactions, adhering to established principles. Given that responsibility is the overarching principle that guides the development of a chatbot, the other sections of privacy, robustness, accountability, reliability, transparency, and inclusiveness all contribute to the idea of a responsible chatbot, but these goals will be addressed in their respective sections. This section will only highlight unique goals that are specific to fulfilling the idea of responsibility. These goals include adhering to ethical principles, content moderation, user support and feedback, and continuous improvement.

C2 Content Moderation

Definition:

Developers should implement measures to prevent and address inappropriate content generated by the chatbot.

Techniques:

  • Content Filters: use automated or manual filters to moderate content.
  • Safety Protocols: develop protocols for addressing harmful or abusive interactions.
  • User Reporting: implement systems for users to report inappropriate content.
