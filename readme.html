Overview

The Guideline is separated into seven major sections. You may choose to filter and find specific information using the Select Section or Select Tag features on the home page, or both.

The tables below outline chapters in the four reference guidelines that correspond to each goal and section. You may refer to the tables to read more about the policies and practices for responsible and ethical AI development.

  • Singapore Computer Society Artificial Intelligence Ethics & Governance Body of Knowledge by Singapore Computer Society
  • Microsoft Responsible AI Standards by Microsoft
  • Model Artificial Intelligence Governance Framework by Personal Data Protection Commission Singapore and AI Verify Foundation
  • Model AI Governance Framework for Generative AI by AI Verify Foundation and Infocomm Media Development Authority

Legend for the Tables

The first part identifies the document or framework, and the second part specifies the chapter, clause, or goal. Examples:

  • SCS 1.0 Chapter 5.2
    Refers to v1.0 Chapter 5.2 in the Singapore Computer Society AI Ethics and Governance Body of Knowledge.
  • GenAI Chapter 2
    Refers to Chapter 2: Data from the Model AI Governance Framework for Generative AI.
  • Microsoft Goal RS1
    Refers to Goal RS1: Reliability and Safety Guidance in the Microsoft Responsible AI Standard.
  • AIGov 3.54
    Refers to Clause 3.54 in the Model Artificial Intelligence Governance Framework.
