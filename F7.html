Section F: Reliability

Reliability in an AI chatbot refers to its consistent performance, accuracy, and dependability in providing information and assistance to users. A reliable chatbot operates effectively under various conditions, maintains high uptime, and delivers accurate and timely responses.

F7 Data Integrity

Definition:

Developers should ensure the integrity of the data the chatbot relies on and processes, maintaining accuracy and consistency.

Techniques:

  • Data Validation: Implement rigorous data validation processes to ensure that the data the chatbot uses is accurate and reliable.
  • Consistency Checks: Regularly check for data consistency across different parts of the system to prevent errors.
  • Secure Data Storage: Use secure methods for data storage and transmission to protect data integrity.
