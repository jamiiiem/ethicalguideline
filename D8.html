Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of developers, organizations, and the chatbot itself to take responsibility for its actions, outcomes, and impacts. This includes ensuring that the chatbot adheres to ethical standards, operates transparently, and addresses any harm or issues that arise from its use.

D8 Ethical Governance

Definition:

Developers should implement governance structures that ensure the chatbot’s development and operations are aligned with ethical standards.

Techniques:

  • Ethics Committees: establish an ethics committee to oversee the chatbot’s ethical compliance and address any concerns.
  • Regular Reviews: conduct regular reviews of the chatbot’s adherence to ethical guidelines and its impact on users.
