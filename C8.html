Section C: Responsibility

The Responsibility section focuses on ensuring that the AI chatbot behaves ethically and responsibly in all interactions, adhering to the principles of fairness, transparency, and user safety. This section outlines how the chatbot should be designed and maintained to take responsibility for its actions and decisions, minimising harm and promoting positive outcomes for users. The goal is to create a chatbot that not only performs its tasks effectively but also contributes to the well-being of its users by acting in a socially and ethically responsible manner.

C8 Continuous Improvement

Definition:

The chatbot’s development should be an ongoing process, involving continuous feedback loops to improve its functionality and ethical behavior.

Techniques:

  • User Feedback: actively collect user feedback to identify areas for improvement.
  • Performance Reviews: schedule regular performance reviews to assess the chatbot’s effectiveness.
  • AI Updates: apply machine learning updates to improve the chatbot’s performance and reduce bias over time.
