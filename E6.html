Section E: Transparency

Transparency in an AI chatbot involves being open and clear about how the chatbot operates, the data it uses, and the decisions it makes. It ensures that users understand the chatbot’s functions, limitations, and the processes behind its responses, fostering trust and accountability.

E6 Ethical Guidelines Disclosure

Definition:

Developers should publish the ethical principles and guidelines that govern the chatbot’s development and operation.

Techniques:

  • Ethical Statement: Publish a clear ethical statement that outlines the principles guiding the chatbot’s design and use.
  • Ethical Reviews: Conduct and publish reviews that assess the chatbot’s adherence to ethical guidelines, sharing the findings with users.
  • Stakeholder Input: Involve users and other stakeholders in discussions about the chatbot’s ethical practices, and transparently incorporate their feedback.
