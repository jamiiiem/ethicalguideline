Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an AI chatbot involve ensuring that the chatbot is accessible and equitable to all users, regardless of their background, demographics, or abilities. It encompasses the elimination of biases and the provision of equal treatment in the chatbot's interactions, promoting diversity and accessibility.

G2 Accessibility

Definition:

Developers should ensure that the chatbot is accessible to users with diverse abilities, including those with disabilities.

Techniques:

  • Accessible Design: Incorporate accessible design principles, such as supporting screen readers, providing alt-text for images, and using high-contrast colors.
  • Multimodal Interaction: Enable interaction through multiple modes, including text, voice, and visual inputs, to accommodate different user preferences and needs.
  • Compliance with Accessibility Standards: Adhere to recognized accessibility standards such as WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) to ensure broad usability.
