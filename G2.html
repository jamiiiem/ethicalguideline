Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an educational AI chatbot involve catering to diverse demographics and actively mitigating biases. This section outlines the specific goals that need to be achieved to best promote inclusiveness and fairness. These goals include bias mitigation, fair decision-making, accessibility and customisation, and diversity.

G2 Fair Decision-Making

Definition:

The chatbot’s decision-making processes should be fair and unbiased. It should not discriminate and should provide equal treatment to all.

Techniques:

  • Fairness Algorithms: implement algorithms to ensure fair outcomes in the chatbot’s decision-making processes.
  • Decision Transparency: ensure that the chatbot can explain its decision-making processes clearly and transparently to users.
  • Equitable Data Use: use data processing methods that avoid reinforcing biases and ensure all users are treated fairly.
