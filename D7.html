Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of developers, organizations, and the chatbot itself to take responsibility for its actions, outcomes, and impacts. This includes ensuring that the chatbot adheres to ethical standards, operates transparently, and addresses any harm or issues that arise from its use.

D7 Clear Role Assignments

Definition:

Developers should establish clear roles and responsibilities among stakeholders involved in the development, deployment, and maintenance of the chatbot.

Techniques:

  • Responsibility Matrix: create a Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RACI) to define who is responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed for each aspect of the chatbot’s lifecycle.
  • Role Documentation: document each role’s duties and expectations to ensure accountability across teams.
