Section C: Responsibility

The Responsibility section focuses on ensuring that the AI chatbot behaves ethically and responsibly in all interactions, adhering to established principles. Given that responsibility is the overarching principle that guides the development of a chatbot, the other sections of privacy, robustness, accountability, reliability, transparency, and inclusiveness all contribute to the idea of a responsible chatbot, but these goals will be addressed in their respective sections. This section will only highlight unique goals that are specific to fulfilling the idea of responsibility. These goals include adhering to ethical principles, content moderation, user support and feedback, and continuous improvement.

C3 User Support and Feedback

Definition:

The chatbot should contain mechanisms for user support and feedback collection to make improvements.

Techniques:

  • Support Channels: offer multiple support channels.
  • Feedback Forms: provide feedback forms for users to share their experiences.
  • Issue Tracking: track and address reported issues in a systematic manner.
  • Grievance Procedures: establish clear procedures for users to lodge complaints.
  • Incident Review: review incidents to improve and prevent recurrence.
  • Dynamic Learning: enable the chatbot to learn from user feedback and adjust its behaviour accordingly.

Examples:

  • Example support channels include in-app support like help desks, chat supports, FAQs, and user guides, as well as external support like support forum, social media community, and email support.
