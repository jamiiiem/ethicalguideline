Section E: Transparency

Transparency in an AI chatbot involves being open and clear about how the chatbot operates, the data it uses, and the decisions it makes. It ensures that users understand the chatbot’s functions, limitations, and the processes behind its responses, fostering trust and accountability.

E3 Data Usage Transparency

Definition:

Developers should clearly communicate how user data is collected, stored, and used by the chatbot.

Techniques:

  • Data Collection Notices: Provide real-time notifications to users whenever their data is being collected, explaining the purpose and scope.
  • Privacy Policy Access: Ensure users can easily access the chatbot’s privacy policy, which should clearly outline data usage practices.
  • User Data Control: Give users control over their data, including the ability to view, modify, or delete their data as needed.
