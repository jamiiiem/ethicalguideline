Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of the developers and the chatbot to accept their responsibilities and face consequences whenever necessary. This section lists the specific goals that need to be achieved to ensure the accountability principles are upheld to a reasonable standard. These goals include clear role assignments, self-monitoring, corrective actions, incident response, and regulatory compliance.

D2 Self-Monitoring

Definition:

The chatbot should be able to monitor its own operations and detect errors or problematic behavior.
