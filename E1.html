Section E: Transparency

Transparency in an AI chatbot involves being open and clear about how the chatbot operates, the data it uses, and the decisions it makes. It ensures that users understand the chatbot’s functions, limitations, and the processes behind its responses, fostering trust and accountability.

E1 Clear Communication of Capabilities

Definition:

Developers should provide users with a clear understanding of what the chatbot can and cannot do.

Techniques:

  • User Guides: Develop user-friendly guides that explain the chatbot’s features, capabilities, and intended use cases.
  • Capability Disclosures: Ensure the chatbot informs users about its limitations and areas of uncertainty during interactions.
  • FAQ Sections: Create and maintain a comprehensive FAQ section that addresses common questions about the chatbot’s abilities and limitations.
