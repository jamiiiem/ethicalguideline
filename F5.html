Section F: Reliability

Reliability in an AI chatbot refers to its consistent performance, accuracy, and dependability in providing information and assistance to users. A reliable chatbot operates effectively under various conditions, maintains high uptime, and delivers accurate and timely responses.

F5 Error Handling and Recovery

Definition:

The chatbot should have the capability to handle errors gracefully and recover quickly from failures.

Techniques:

  • Graceful Degradation: Design the chatbot to continue operating in a reduced capacity when parts of the system fail, rather than crashing entirely.
  • Error Recovery Protocols: Implement protocols that allow the chatbot to recover from errors and resume normal operation without user disruption.
  • User-Friendly Error Messages: Provide clear and helpful error messages that guide users on how to proceed when something goes wrong.
  • Automated Error Logging: Implement automated systems to log and address errors in real-time.
  • Error Notification: Notify users promptly if an error occurs, with clear instructions or alternative resources.
