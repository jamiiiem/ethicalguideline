Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an AI chatbot involve ensuring that the chatbot is accessible and equitable to all users, regardless of their background, demographics, or abilities. It encompasses the elimination of biases and the provision of equal treatment in the chatbot's interactions, promoting diversity and accessibility.

G3 Cultural Sensitivity

Definition:

Developers should ensure that the chatbot is culturally sensitive and respectful in its interactions with users from different backgrounds.

Techniques:
