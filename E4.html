Section E: Transparency

Transparency in an AI chatbot involves being open and clear about how the chatbot operates, the data it uses, and the decisions it makes. It ensures that users understand the chatbot’s functions, limitations, and the processes behind its responses, fostering trust and accountability.

E4 Algorithmic Transparency

Definition:

Developers should make the chatbot’s underlying algorithms and decision-making processes accessible and understandable to users.

Techniques:

  • Algorithm Summaries: Provide summaries or simplified explanations of the algorithms used by the chatbot, including any AI models.
  • Open-Source Components: Where possible, use open-source algorithms or provide access to certain components of the chatbot’s codebase.
  • Transparent Model Training: Offer insights into how the chatbot’s models are trained, including the data sources and training processes used.
