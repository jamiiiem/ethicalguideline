Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an AI chatbot involve ensuring that the chatbot is accessible and equitable to all users, regardless of their background, demographics, or abilities. It encompasses the elimination of biases and the provision of equal treatment in the chatbot's interactions, promoting diversity and accessibility.

G7 Inclusive User Testing

Definition:

Developers should conduct testing and validation with a diverse group of users to ensure the chatbot performs well across different demographics.

Techniques:

  • Diverse User Groups: Involve users from various backgrounds, including those with different abilities, ethnicities, and ages, in the testing process.
  • Feedback Analysis: Collect and analyze feedback from diverse users to identify and address any inclusivity or fairness issues.
