Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of developers, organizations, and the chatbot itself to take responsibility for its actions, outcomes, and impacts. This includes ensuring that the chatbot adheres to ethical standards, operates transparently, and addresses any harm or issues that arise from its use.

D10 Transparency and Reporting

Definition:

Developers should ensure transparency in the chatbot’s operations, decision-making processes, and any corrective actions taken when issues arise.

Techniques:

  • Transparency Reports: publish regular reports detailing the chatbot’s performance, incidents, and corrective measures.
  • User Notifications: notify users of any significant changes, issues, or updates to the chatbot that might affect their experience.
