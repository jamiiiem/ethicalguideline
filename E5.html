Section E: Transparency

Transparency in an AI chatbot involves being open and clear about how the chatbot operates, the data it uses, and the decisions it makes. It ensures that users understand the chatbot’s functions, limitations, and the processes behind its responses, fostering trust and accountability.

E5 Regular Transparency Reports

Definition:

Developers should publish regular reports that provide insights into the chatbot’s operations, performance, and any significant changes.

Techniques:

  • Performance Metrics: Include metrics on accuracy, response times, and error rates in transparency reports to give users a clear picture of the chatbot’s performance.
  • Incident Reporting: Document and publish any significant incidents, including how they were handled and what corrective actions were taken.
  • User Feedback Summaries: Provide summaries of user feedback and how it has been used to improve the chatbot.
