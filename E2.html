E2 Explainable AI (XAI)

Definition:

Developers should ensure that the chatbot can explain the reasoning behind its decisions and actions in a way that users can understand.

Techniques:

  • Decision Explanations: Enable the chatbot to provide explanations for its decisions, allowing users to understand the logic and data used.
  • Transparency Layers: Develop a transparency layer within the chatbot that gives users the option to see how certain decisions were made.
  • Interactive Queries: Allow users to ask the chatbot for more detailed explanations of specific responses, enhancing understanding.
