Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of developers, organizations, and the chatbot itself to take responsibility for its actions, outcomes, and impacts. This includes ensuring that the chatbot adheres to ethical standards, operates transparently, and addresses any harm or issues that arise from its use.

D5 Ethical Boundaries Enforcement

Definition:

The chatbot should have the responsibility to enforce ethical boundaries during interactions, avoiding harmful or inappropriate content.

Techniques:

  • Ethical Filters: incorporate filters that automatically block or flag content that may violate ethical standards.
  • Real-Time Moderation: equip the chatbot with real-time moderation capabilities to enforce boundaries during conversations.
  • Ethical Alerts: develop systems where the chatbot can alert users if a conversation is approaching unethical territory.
