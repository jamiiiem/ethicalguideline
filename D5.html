Section D: Accountability

Accountability in AI chatbot development refers to the obligation of the developers and the chatbot to accept their responsibilities and face consequences whenever necessary. This section lists the specific goals that need to be achieved to ensure the accountability principles are upheld to a reasonable standard. These goals include clear role assignments, self-monitoring, corrective actions, incident response, and regulatory compliance.

D5 Regulatory Compliance

Definition:

Developers should adhere to relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards to ensure the chatbot operates within legal frameworks.

Techniques:

  • Legal Audits: conduct regular legal audits to maintain compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Compliance Training: provide ongoing training for developers on relevant legal and regulatory requirements.

Considerations:

  • Developers should ensure third-party audit services comply with all local regulations specific to the region.
