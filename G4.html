Section G: Inclusiveness and Fairness

Inclusiveness and Fairness in an educational AI chatbot involve catering to diverse demographics and actively mitigating biases. This section outlines the specific goals that need to be achieved to best promote inclusiveness and fairness. These goals include bias mitigation, fair decision-making, accessibility and customisation, and diversity.

G4 Diversity

Definition:

Developers should actively involve diverse users and ensure that the chatbot can interact with a diverse user base.

Techniques:

  • Cultural Sensitivity Practice: ensure that the chatbot is culturally sensitive in its interactions with users from different backgrounds.
  • Diverse Representation: ensure diverse representation in the chatbot’s responses to reflect the diversity of its user base.
  • Inclusive User Testing: involve users from various backgrounds in the testing process and the feedback collection process.

Examples:

  • Practices to cultivate cultural sensitivity include cultural training, using data that reflects diverse cultural norms, languages, and expressions; localisation, tailoring the chatbot’s responses to be relevant and appropriate to users in specific regions and cultures; and language support, providing support for multiple languages and dialects.
  • To achieve diverse representation, the chatbot can create content representing various demographics, train with scenarios involving diverse characters, and incorporate feedback from diverse user groups to continuously improve representation.
