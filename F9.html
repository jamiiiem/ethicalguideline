Section F: Reliability

Reliability in an AI chatbot refers to its consistent performance, accuracy, and dependability in providing information and assistance to users. A reliable chatbot operates effectively under various conditions, maintains high uptime, and delivers accurate and timely responses.

F9 Feedback Integration

Definition:

The chatbot should incorporate user feedback to continuously improve its performance and reliability.

Techniques:

  • Feedback Loops: Create feedback loops where user input is analysed and used to refine the chatbot.
  • Adaptive Learning: Employ adaptive learning models that evolve based on user interactions and feedback.
